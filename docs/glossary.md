# Glossary

- **Self**: Archetype of psychic totality that integrates conscious and unconscious dimensions.
- **Ego**: Center of conscious awareness and identity organization.
- **Shadow**: Disowned or unintegrated aspects of personality and psyche.
- **Anima**: Inner feminine psychic image in the masculine psyche (in classical Jungian framing).
- **Animus**: Inner masculine psychic image in the feminine psyche (in classical Jungian framing).
- **Syzygy**: Dynamic pairing of opposites (often anima/animus) toward psychic balance.
- **Individuation**: Lifelong process of integrating opposites into a more whole personality.
- **Archetype**: Recurring primordial pattern or organizing image in the psyche.
- **Pleroma**: Symbolic fullness containing undifferentiated opposites.
- **Psychopomp**: Guide figure mediating transitions between psychic levels.
- **Coniunctio**: Alchemical-symbolic union of opposites.
- **Quaternity**: Fourfold symbolic structure associated with wholeness.
