Key Concepts from Jung's Aion

This page offers short explanations of the main ideas explored throughout Jung's text.

  • Self – the totality of the psyche and the goal of individuation.
  • Shadow – the unconscious aspects of the personality often projected onto others.
  • Anima/Animus – the contrasexual archetypes that bridge consciousness and the unconscious.
  • Trickster – the chaotic aspect that disrupts and renews psychological structures.
