Key Concepts

This quick reference summarizes central archetypes in Jung's Aion.

  • Self – the organizing totality of the psyche that seeks wholeness.
  • Shadow – the repressed and unrecognized aspects of the personality.
  • Anima/Animus – the inner contrasexual image guiding relationship to the opposite sex.
  • Trickster – the chaotic energy that disrupts complacency and provokes transformation.
