Essential Concepts from Aion

This primer summarizes Jung's main ideas so you can grasp the book's message at a glance.

  • The Ego – the center of conscious identity.
  • The Shadow – hidden or rejected aspects of the psyche.
  • Anima/Animus – the soul-image representing contrasexual qualities.
  • The Self – the totality of the psyche aiming for wholeness.
  • Christ as Symbol – an expression of the Self archetype in history.
  • Age of Pisces – the era characterized by a split between light and dark.
  • Integration – the task of uniting conscious and unconscious parts.
