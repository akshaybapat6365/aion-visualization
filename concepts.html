Key Concepts in Jung's Aion

The Self

Archetypal wholeness that embraces both conscious and unconscious aspects of personality.

The Shadow

The rejected or unconscious side of the personality that often appears projected onto others.

Anima/Animus

The inner contrasexual figure that mediates between consciousness and the deeper psyche.

The Trickster

A disruptive yet transformative force that reveals hidden truths through paradox and humor.
