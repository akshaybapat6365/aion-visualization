About

Aion Visualization

A modern, interactive companion for Carl Jung's Aion. Built to support careful reading, non-linear exploration, and meaningful reflection.

What This Is

Aion Visualization is a visual learning environment: chapters are rendered through a consistent chapter shell; concepts and symbols are centralized into canonical datasets; and explorations are designed to teach, not just decorate.

How to Use

  • Start with Chapters for a guided path through the book's arc.
  • Use the Atlas to see cross-links and clusters, then jump into any chapter.
  • Open the Glossary drawer on any page to ground definitions consistently.

Scholarly Integrity

This project avoids invented quotations and treats symbols as psychological, not merely aesthetic. Interpretations are kept conservative and are designed to invite the reader back to the text.

Core Modes

  • Journey: consistent chapter contract with reflection and next links.
  • Atlas: connected view of chapters and motifs.
  • Glossary: canonical definitions, deep links, and search.

Design Targets

Consistency: one typographic system, tokenized spacing, shared components.
Performance: route budgets enforced in CI, heavy visualizations lazy-init on user intent.
Accessibility: keyboard-first navigation and reduced-motion support.
