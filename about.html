A Visual Companion
to Jung's Aion

Fourteen immersive Three.js scenes — each chapter rendered as a full-screen experience where symbols breathe, orbit, and respond to your presence.

◉

Visualization-First

Every chapter opens on its visualization. Text lives in a collapsible sidebar — the art is the primary experience.

☽

Psychological Symbols

Shadow mirrors, alchemical stages, mandalas, and ouroboros rings. Each scene encodes the chapter's core symbolism.

⊕

Interactive Exploration

Move your mouse to activate elements. Scroll to zoom. The visualizations respond to your engagement.

⚗

Scholarly Integrity

Interpretations stay grounded. The goal is to invite you back to the primary text, not to replace it.

Design Principles

Consistency

One typographic system, tokenized spacing, shared language across all pages.

Performance

Static deployment, lazy-init visualizations, under 200ms load.

Depth

Fourteen unique scenes, each with distinct palettes, mechanics, and symbolism.

Built With

Three.js WebGL D3.js Playfair Display ES Modules Vercel
