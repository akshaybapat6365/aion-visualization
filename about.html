About

Aion Visualization is an immersive companion to Carl Jung's Aion: Researches into the Phenomenology of the Self. Each of the fourteen chapters is rendered as a full-screen interactive Three.js visualization — ego points, shadow mirrors, alchemical transformations, and mandalas that respond to your presence. Text lives in a collapsible sidebar, not overlaid on the art.

The project treats symbols as psychological realities rather than decorations. Interpretations stay conservative; the goal is to invite you back to the text, not replace it.
