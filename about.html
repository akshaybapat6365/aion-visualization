About Aion Visualization

The Project

Aion Visualization is an interactive exploration of Carl Jung's masterwork "Aion: Researches into the Phenomenology of the Self." This digital experience transforms Jung's complex psychological concepts into visual, interactive journeys that make his profound insights accessible to modern audiences.

Through six carefully crafted visualizations and comprehensive chapter guides, we invite you to explore the depths of the human psyche as Jung understood it - from the ego and shadow to the ultimate goal of individuation and the Self.

"The privilege of a lifetime is to become who you truly are." - C.G. Jung

Why Aion?

Published in 1951, "Aion" represents Jung's mature reflection on the evolution of human consciousness through the lens of Christian and Gnostic symbolism. The book traces how the symbol of Christ has evolved over 2,000 years, paralleling humanity's psychological development.

Jung believed we are living in a time of profound transition - moving from the Age of Pisces to the Age of Aquarius. This shift demands a new understanding of the relationship between conscious and unconscious, individual and collective, light and shadow.

Interactive Features

  • Shadow Integration: Experience the process of integrating rejected aspects of the self
  • Anima/Animus Constellation: Explore the relationships between contrasexual archetypes
  • Fish Symbol Timeline: Journey through 2,500 years of symbolic evolution
  • Gnostic Cosmology: Navigate the hierarchical realms of ancient wisdom
  • Alchemical Laboratory: Transform base matter into gold through psychological opus
  • Aion Clock: Witness the precession of equinoxes and the turning of ages

Technical Implementation

Built with modern web technologies including Three.js, Matter.js, D3.js, and GSAP. The visualizations are designed to work seamlessly across devices, bringing Jung's insights to life through interactive experiences.
