Chapter Summaries

1. The Ego

Jung describes the conscious personality and its limits.

2. The Shadow

Explores the dark, unacknowledged aspects of the psyche.

3. The Syzygy

The pairing of Anima and Animus as mediators to the unconscious.

4. The Self

Introduction of the Self as a regulating center aiming for wholeness.

5. Christ as Symbol of the Self

Historical expression of the Self archetype through the figure of Christ.

6. The Sign of the Fishes

Discusses the astrological background of the Christian era.

7. The Historical Significance of the Fish

Surveys fish symbolism across cultures leading into alchemical motifs.

8. The Coniunctio

Final chapter detailing the integration of opposites.
